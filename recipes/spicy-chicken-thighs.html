Spicy Chicken Thighs

Serves 1–2 | Air Fryer Temp: 180°C

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Thighs (medium or large)
  • 1 Garlic Clove, finely chopped
  • 1 Lemon, cut into 2 thick slices
  • Salt & Black Pepper (generous on the salt)
  • Paprika
  • Onion Powder

Note: For 4 chicken thighs, remember to double the garlic and the lemon.

Instructions

  1. The Lemon Base: Place the chicken thighs in the air fryer basket skin-side down. Squeeze one thick lemon slice over each thigh, then throw the squeezed lemon pieces into the basket with the chicken.
  2. Seasoning (Side 1): Sprinkle the chopped garlic over the thighs. Lightly cover both with salt, pepper, paprika, and onion powder.
  3. The First Cook:
    • Medium thighs: Cook for 12 minutes.
    • Large thighs: Cook for 14 minutes.
  4. The Flip: Turn the thighs over so they are skin-side up.
  5. Seasoning (Side 2): Lightly cover the skin side with salt, pepper, paprika, and onion powder.
  6. The Final Cook:
    • Medium thighs: Cook for a final 13 minutes (25 mins total).
    • Large thighs: Cook for a final 15 minutes (29 mins total).

Why this works

By starting skin-side down with the lemon and garlic, you’re infusing the meat side with flavor while the fat begins to render. Finishing skin-side up for the longer stretch ensures the skin gets that perfect, spicy crunch without burning the garlic.
